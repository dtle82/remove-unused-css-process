HOW DO YOU GET STARTED?

SO GLAD YOU ASKED. START HERE.
Get Clever with Your Wireless Service

Mint Mobile took what’s wrong with wireless and made it right. Unlike the traditional Big Wireless and prepaid plans you’re used to, our phone plans give you more flexibility and value, plus the blazing fast nationwide 4G LTE coverage and unlimited talk and text you expect. You don’t need a fancy (*cough* expensive) new phone, either—just grab a new Mint Mobile GSM SIM card and you’re ready to go!

More Freedom

Mint Mobile is online-only. Our all digital footprint means you can shop for wireless from the comfort of your home. No more waiting in stores, dodging those pesky salespeople. Oh, and no sneaky phone gimmicks. Keep your phone and your phone number—we’re BYOP, baby! That means you can bring your own phone to Mint instead of having to buy or lease a new one! Just make sure it’s GSM compatible and it’ll work great when you drop in a Mint Mobile SIM card (P.S. you can buy a new phone from us if you want, but that’s not really what we’re all about—unlike all those bigger retailers).

More Value

We’ve made the ‘warehouse club’ model work for wireless. We bundle our plans together into 3-, 6- and 12-month packages, giving you bulk discounts on the coverage you buy. The more months you commit to, the more you save—with prices as low as just $15 a month!

Big Features & Bulk Savings

All Mint Mobile plans include nationwide unlimited talk and text (to Canada and Mexico too!) plus your choice of 3GB, 8GB, or 12GB of 4G LTE high-speed data per month, so you aren’t overpaying for unlimited 4G LTE you may not even need (some studies show the average American only uses about 5GB per month). And if you use all of your LTE data allotment, no worries, we don’t charge overages. Check out our prepaid phone plans to find the one that best meets your needs and start saving like a fox on wireless with Mint Mobile!

Risk-Free

We think fate brought us together, but we know things don’t always work out. That’s why all of our plans come with an unconditional 7-day money back guarantee. So try us out—you’ve got nothing to lose, except all that money your forking over to Big Wireless each month.
